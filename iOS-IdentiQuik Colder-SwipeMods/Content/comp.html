Powerful advantages over barcodes and RFID labels

Whether you're comparing IdentiQuik® couplings to barcodes or adhesive RFID labels, there is really no comparison in terms of functionality and long-term value. Learn more about the many advantages of our smart couplings now.

Advantages over barcodes

Barcodes may have a lower purchase price, but when you compare the many advantages of IdentiQuik® couplings over this static technology, the choice is clear for achieving the highest value from your investment.

IdentiQuik advantages over barcodes include:
  • Rewriteability
  • Network independence
  • Higher data capacity
  • Greater security
  • Greater durability
  • Simpler orientation

Advantages over RFID labels

Despite leveraging the same communication technology, IdentiQuik® couplings offer several functionality and security capabilities that cannot be achieved with adhesive RFID labels.

IdentiQuik advantages over RFID labels include:
  • Greater durability
  • Easier configuration
  • Greater convenience
  • Stronger integration
  • Simpler orientation
