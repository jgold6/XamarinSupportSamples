IdentiQuik® advantage over barcodes:

Frustrate counterfeiters

Compared to barcodes, the larger data capacity of the IdentiQuik® couplings provides space for a strong data authentication code. IdentiQuik couplings are very challenging to counterfeit, especially when compared to barcodes that can be replicated with a copier. For data privacy, the controller may be set up to encrypt the data written to the tag.
