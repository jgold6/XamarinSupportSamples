IdentiQuik® Universal Dispensing Coupler (UDC) Specifications

Body with reader

  • Polypropylene flow path
  • 316 stainless steel spring
  • EPDM o-rings
  • 1/4 in. – 3/4 in. (6.35 mm – 19.05 mm) hose barb terminations
  • Inline
  • Operating range: 8V – 24V
  • Multi-drop RS-232 or USB interface
  • 2-ft. (60 cm) cable with 9-pin D-sub or USB connector

Insert with tag

  • Polypropylene or acetal flow path
  • 302 stainless steel spring
  • EPDM o-ring
  • 1.5-in. (38 mm) female thread or snap-in termination
  • Inline
  • Valved
  • ISO-15693, 112 bytes programmable
